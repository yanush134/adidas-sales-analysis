## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes Adidas sales performance in 2022, focusing on revenue, profit, product performance, retailer performance, and sales methods.

The goal was to transform raw sales data into an interactive Tableau dashboard that provides a clear overview of business performance and helps identify key sales and profitability trends.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The main objectives of this analysis were to:

- Evaluate overall sales and profit performance
- Analyze monthly sales and profit trends
- Identify the best-performing retailers
- Compare sales and profit across product categories
- Analyze retailer sales methods
- Identify the highest and lowest performing periods
- Create an interactive dashboard for business performance monitoring

## 🔎 Key Insights

### 📅 Monthly Performance

- The highest monthly sales reached approximately **$12.2M**.
- The highest monthly profit reached approximately **$4.8M**.
- The lowest monthly sales were approximately **$6.9M**.
- The lowest monthly profit was approximately **$2.6M**.
- Sales and profit showed similar trends throughout the year, indicating a strong relationship between revenue growth and profitability.

### 🏪 Retailer Performance

- **West Gear** was the top-performing retailer with approximately **$32M in sales** and **$12.2M in profit**.
- **Foot Locker** generated approximately **$29M in sales** and **$11.3M in profit**.
- **Sports Direct** generated approximately **$25M in sales** and **$10.6M in profit**.
- **Amazon** had the lowest overall sales among the analyzed retailers at approximately **$10M**.

### 👟 Product Performance

- **Men's Street Footwear** was the best-performing product category with approximately **$28M in sales** and **$11.6M in profit**.
- **Women's Apparel** generated approximately **$24M in sales** and **$9.7M in profit**.
- **Women's Athletic Footwear** showed the lowest performance among the analyzed categories with approximately **$14M in sales** and **$5.6M in profit**.

### 🛒 Sales Methods

The dashboard also compares the distribution of sales methods across six major retailers:

- Amazon
- Foot Locker
- Kohl's
- Sports Direct
- Walmart
- West Gear

This analysis helps evaluate how different retailers distribute their sales across **Outlet, Online, and In-Store** channels.
